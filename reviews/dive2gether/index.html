Dive2gether

25 April 2023 by Robin

  • diving
  • padi
  • plakias

Dive2gether in Plakias is a PADI 5* Instructor Development Center. They offer a variety of recreational activities including shore diving, boat diving, snorkelling, night snorkelling/diving and a weekly BBQ.

The format for the day’s activities was much as you might expect:

  • Meet at the Dive Center for introductions, kit checks and packing
  • Travel to the port (for boat dives) or beach (for shore dives)
  • Arrive at the dive site.
  • Briefing for the dive.
  • Into the water with your group for the first dive.
  • Out of the water for drinks, lunch and a relax.
  • Briefing for the dive.
  • Into the water with your group for the second dive.
  • Back on the boat to disassemble kit and make the short trip back to port.

The setup is very good, the staff are very friendly and the kit is all well-maintained.

[Dive2gether][1] in Plakias is a PADI 5* Instructor Development Center. They offer a variety of recreational activities including shore diving, boat diving, snorkelling, night snorkelling/diving and a weekly BBQ.

Having visited them for a look around back in April, we booked a couple of boat dives last week when we were in Plakias for a beach-day with visiting relatives. As we were going to be using our own equipment, they advised us they would want to test our BCDs and regulators.

We arrived yesterday morning at 08:30 and met our guides for the day, Linda (who is part of the Marine Biology Research team) and Electra (who is currently on their Bootcamp programme). After checking our equipment, we packed everything into personal crates for the short trip to the port. Arriving at the boat, everyone helped load the tanks, crates and other necessities. After setting up our equipment and a quick welcome from the boat captain, we all introduced ourselves before setting off for the first dive site.

The format for the day's activities was much as you might expect:

- Arrive at the dive site.
- Briefing for the dive.
- Into the water with your group for the first dive.
- Back on the boat for drinks, lunch and a relax while moving to the second dive site.
- Briefing for the dive.
- Into the water with your group for the second dive.
- Back on the boat to disassemble kit and make the short trip back to port.

Again, once we were back in port, everyone helped unloading the equipment and we were soon back at Dive2gether filling in our logbooks.

We dived Willy’s Wall in Damnoni Bay and Paligremnos Wall in Plakias Bay. After the variety of sea life we saw, we will definitely be back for more diving here; sea slugs, grouper, ray, purple frilly Flabellina, loads of lion fish, scorpionfish and hermit crab.

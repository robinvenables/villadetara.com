The Greek Golden Visa Program grants a five-year residence permit in exchange for property investment in Greece as low as a €250,000.  You can include your family in the residency program.

We had a meeting this morning with our property lawyer in preparatuiomwe went to Chania to have our fingerprints taken electronically for our Greek biometric residency cards.

![Villa de Tara](/images/sunrise.jpeg)

Villa de Tara is a 3 bedroom, 3 bathroom villa with amazing sea views and a private pool just outside the village of Kefalas.

The ground floor is open-plan with patio doors from the lounge overlooking the terrace and the infinity pool. There is a double bedroom on this floor with a good sized shower room, while upstairs there are a further 2 double bedrooms, one of which is ensuite, and a separate bathroom.

Outside the villa faces the sea to the east with amazing views of the sunrise and out towards Rethymnon. The pool is 30m² and there is an outdoor shower, kitchen and BBQ area next to it, as well as sunbathing and terraced areas. On the stepped levels below there are almond, cherry, chestnut, olive, orange and walnut trees.
